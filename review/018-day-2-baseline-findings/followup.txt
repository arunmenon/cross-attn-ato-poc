2. Blocker: sklearn threshold-cliff makes HN-FPR unfair across models.

Status: FIXED.

Action:
  - eval/score_risk.py: NEW tie-aware exact-target legit-FPR formula
    with FRACTIONAL `need = target_fpr * n_legit` (review 019
    Blocker 1; rounding fails the 1e-4 acceptance on small clean-eval
    n_legit). hard_negative_fpr accepts (threshold, alpha) and weights
    tied-at-threshold rows by alpha so achieved_fpr == target exactly.
  - eval/bootstrap_ci.py: bootstrap_hard_negative_fpr per-resample
    recomputes (threshold, alpha). CI bundle gains tie_fraction,
    achieved_fpr, threshold, alpha (point + mean across resamples).
  - The corrected leaderboard (clean eval, tie-aware):
    structured_as_text 0.0507 > xattn_smoke 0.0537 > lora_text 0.0701
    > event_only 0.0730. Reverses the apparent ranking from sklearn's
    cliff.
